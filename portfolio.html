wash' a meteor sh...ower?

Radiant, glorious and sometimes deadly, comets and meteorites might be one of natures most
riveting creations. they appear individually or in a stream of cosmic debris hurtling down
towards the Earth. this event is called a meteor shower: recurring approximately at
the same date each year, they vary in speed frequency and radiance. This page's main goal is to
introduce you to the most common meteor showers.

a list of the most common meteor showers

  1. Alpha Capricornids:

    2. The radiant of the Alpha Capricornids is near to the constellation Capricornus, from
    which the shower gets its name. Patience is required
    in order to observe this meteor shower. the 2021 Alpha Capricornids meteor shower will probably produce the greatest number of
    meteors on the mornings of july 29, 30 and 31.

  2. Perseids

    3. In the Northern Hemisphere, we rank the August Perseids as our all-time favorite meteor
    shower. The Perseids take place during the lazy, hazy
    days of northern summer, when many families are on vacation.
    the 2021 Perseid meteor shower will probably produce the greatest number of
    meteors on the mornings of August 11, 12 and 13.
